# Villa Adora Knowledge Base — Supplementary Sections (June 2026)

## Pet-Friendly Villa Adora

### Pet Policy
- Pets welcome on request — €35 per pet per night
- Maximum 2 pets per suite
- Pet beds, bowls, and treats provided upon arrival

### Pet Services
- Pet-sitting services available through the concierge
- Veterinary clinic in Bled (5 min drive)
- Pet-friendly hiking: Vintgar Gorge, lakeside path, Bled Castle park
- Pets welcome on the terrace (garden dining area)
- Pets not permitted in the restaurant

## Business Travel & Workation

### Workspace Amenities
- Dedicated workspace in every suite (desk, ergonomic chair, power outlets)
- High-speed fiber optic WiFi (up to 300 Mbps) throughout
- Printing and scanning available at reception
- Quiet outdoor workspaces on the terrace and garden

### Workation Packages
- 5+ night stays include: airport transfer, daily breakfast, wellness access
- Long-stay discounts: 15% off for 7+ nights, 20% off for 14+ nights
- Coworking space at Bled Innovation Hub (10 min drive)
